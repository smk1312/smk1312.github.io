Newpark WorldWise Global Schools

What is WorldWise Global Schools?

WorldWise Global Schools (WWGS) is a new program being introduced to secodary schools throughout the country. It began in 2013 with the aim to promote and improve sustainability in learning environments. And has even reached a global scale, having lots of overseas partnerships and sponsors! It follows the basis of the Sustainable Development Goals in order to reach and attain all pillars of sustainability. If you want further information on the organisation, make sure to check out the WWGS website.

Newpark has consistently taken eco-friendly measures, however it wasn’t until September 2023 that an avid sustainability committee was set up. It is run by Ms. Dempsey and plenty of eager transition year students. Since its origin, Newpark’s WWGS have been promoting the Goals throughout the school and raising awareness for the whole campaign.

The group meets every Tuesday where they come up with creative plans to improve the school community. They take on a new goal every couple months and address where it can be developed in Newpark. You can see below which aspects the committee have focused on, and the actions they’ve taken in the effort to reach and maintain a sustainable future.
